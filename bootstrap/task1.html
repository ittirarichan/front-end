FaceBook

Facebook is a website which allows users, who sign-up for free profiles, to connect with friends, work colleagues or people they don't know, online. It allows users to share pictures, music, videos, and articles, as well as their own thoughts and opinions with however many people they like.

WhatsApp

Meta provides the generative AI technology that powers AI experiences on WhatsApp. Learn about AI experiences on WhatsApp in this article. Note: This feature is currently only available in limited countries and might not be available to you yet, even if other users in your country have access.

Instagram

Instagram is a free photo and video sharing app available on iPhone and Android. People can upload photos or videos to our service and share them with their followers or with a select group of friends. They can also view, comment and like posts shared by their friends on Instagram.
